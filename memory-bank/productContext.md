# Product Context

## Why this project exists
QuizSpark is a study platform where students can use AI to generate questions from their materials and track their learning progress. It aims to make studying more interactive and efficient by leveraging AI to transform study materials into actionable practice questions.

## User Stories
- Students can upload study materials (like PDFs) and use AI to generate practice questions.
- Students can practice with AI-generated questions or manually created question banks.
- Users can track their progress and study history over time.
- Users can interact with an AI chatbot for immediate study assistance.
- Users can collaborate in classrooms and share educational resources.

## Problem Domain
- Online education and self-study.
- AI-assisted learning.
- Educational resource management (question banks, documents).
- Classroom collaboration.
